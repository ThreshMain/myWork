HTML5 Quiz WTF

The Impossible Quiz is a quiz that is... impossible. The answers might not seem to make sense, but, to someone at least, they do! This game requires a special type of mind to complete, and you must also have a great memory too! Be sure to remember your answers as you only get three lives each turn and once you have used those three lives you must start from the beginning again. The answers and questions remain the same however so this is why memory is key!
SHOW MORE

The questions have no logic and you must really think about the potential answer, for example, one of the questions is: 'Can a match box?' and the answer is 'No, but a tin can' (matchbox and tin can get it). You will find yourself both frustrated and addicted to this game. How far can you progress and how many impossible questions can you answer?

Instructions

How to play The Impossible Quiz

Use your mouse to select the right answers. You have 3 lives throughout the quiz. If you answer incorrectly, you will lose one. Lose all 3 lives and it's Game Over.
